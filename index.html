Baking A Cake

  1. Grease a 9-inch round pan
  2. Prepare your ingredients
    • Butter
    • Baking Powder
    • Flour
    • Salt
    • Sugar
    • Eggs
    • Vanilla Extract
    • Milk
  3. Whisk the dry ingredients together
  4. Mix sugar and butter
  5. Add eggs
  6. Combine all ingredients
  7. Pour the batter into your pan
  8. Place the pan in the oven and bake
